About KatieConf

KatieConf is a parody. Have you checked a calendar lately? There is no June 31st. Sorry. The town of Katherine does exist, though.

However, all the people featured in the KatieConf lineup are real. There are so many fantastic female technical presenters who just happen to just happened to be called Katie, or a variation thereupon.

If you are looking for speakers for your conference, why not ask one of the wonerful people featured here? They are all listed with their contact details and examples of their previous talks.

Do you really need another conference lineup of all men? Or as many female speakers as speakers called Steve?
